our story

Veracity Enterprises is a family-owned company based in Midrand, South Africa. It was founded in 2021 by Joel Adediran and specializes in acquiring houses for renting, buying, and selling with a focus on providing high-quality properties at competitive prices. The company has quickly established a reputation for excellence among local buyers and renters. In addition, Veracity Enterprises has an investment in Nigeria, including an interior decoration store, farm, home, and land, which allows the company to provide clients with a more comprehensive range of services, including interior design and agricultural investment opportunities. The company has a key strength in its business center in the heart of Midrand's business district, this state-of-the-art facility provides businesses with the resources they need to thrive, and virtual office services and many more business needs. Under the leadership of Joel Adediran, the company is committed to providing clients with the highest level of service and expertise. With its strong reputation, diverse portfolio, and forward-thinking approach, Veracity Enterprises is well-positioned for continued success in the years to come.

Veracity Enterprises has a strong commitment to sustainability and community engagement, regularly participating in initiatives and projects aimed at improving the lives of residents in the areas where they operate

Veracity Enterprises diversified portfolio allows them to offer a more comprehensive range of services, including investment opportunities and business center services.
